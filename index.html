Hello, I’m Jordan!

I’m a junior studying Computer Science with a minor in Computational Biology at the University of Southern California. I’m passionate about the intersection between biology and computer science. My interests primarily lie in genetics, genomics, and artificial intelligence. In my free time, I love bullet journaling, playing with my cat, making ice cream, and hiking!

Currently, I’m analyzing genotype imputation disparities across diverse populations and developing a fast-shot GAN that stylizes selfies. On the side, I’m reading about creating vision and language navigation (VLN) agents and using deep learning to predict polygenetic risk scores (PRS) and phenotype imputation.
